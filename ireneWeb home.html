Hey there, I’m Irene! Originally from Taiwan, I’ve spent years immersed in music, but now I’m channeling my passion for creativity into graphic design. Although I’m new to the field, I bring a musician’s eye for detail and a love for simplicity to every project. For me, design is all about purpose—each color, layout, and element is carefully chosen to create something meaningful, just like a piece of music. I’m excited to bring my unique perspective to the world of design, combining fresh ideas with thoughtful execution.
