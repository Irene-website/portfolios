Who I am & What I do

Hi, I’m Irene Tsai! I’m a budding web designer with a passion for creating clean,
functional layouts and harmonizing colors to enhance user experiences.

What Makes Me Unique?

My background in music taught me discipline, creativity, and the importance of structure
— all qualities I now bring to my design work.

 

 

Where I Started

I moved to the U.S. at 17, barely knowing English.
Since then, I’ve graduated with a degree in Piano Performance and am now pursuing my passion for graphic design.

 

Where I am Going

Currently, I’m studying Interactive Design and am eager to grow into a skilled web designer who specializes in creating visually striking layouts and intuitive designs

 

 

What I Bring to the Table

⬗ Layout and color design ⬖
⬗ Adobe Creative Suite ⬖
⬗ Google Workspace tools ⬖
⬗ Bilingual: English & Mandarin ⬖

 

What Sets Me Apart

People often describe me as detail-oriented, productive, and a great problem-solver. I also value positivity and humor, which help me tackle challenges creatively.

 

 

Fun Fact

When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me at the piano analysing a piece.

 

Why Design Matters to Me

I love how design brings ideas to life and how a thoughtful layout can make information accessible and engaging.
